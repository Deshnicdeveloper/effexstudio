Background & Rationale

A unifying platform for Cameroon's creative economy.

Cameroon's creative sector — spanning digital content, advertising, marketing, fashion, culinary arts, graphic design, experience design and the arts — is one of the country's fastest-growing sources of youth-led enterprise and cultural expression.

Yet creators, entrepreneurs and storytellers still lack a unifying platform to connect with policymakers, investors, brands and one another. The Cameroon Creative Convention responds to this gap: a single-day, high-visibility gathering that brings the ecosystem together to chart a shared path for the sector's growth.

Objectives

Why we're gathering

Advocate

Position Cameroon's creative economy as a credible driver of national development and job creation.

Build Capacity

Equip creators and creative entrepreneurs with practical business, production and monetization skills.

Facilitate Networking

Connect creators with mentors, investors, brands, media and public institutions.

Showcase Excellence

Celebrate outstanding creative talent and encourage healthy, sustainable competition in the sector.
