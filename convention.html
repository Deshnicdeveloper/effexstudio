Background & Rationale

A unifying platform for Cameroon's creative economy.

Cameroon's creative sector — spanning digital content, advertising, marketing, fashion, culinary arts, graphic design and the arts — is one of the country's fastest-growing sources of youth-led enterprise and cultural expression.

Yet creators still lack a unifying platform to connect with policymakers, investors and brands. CCC 2026 responds to this gap: a single-day, high-visibility gathering that brings the ecosystem together to chart a shared path forward.

Objectives
