More than a school. A launchpad.

Effex Academy exists because talent without training is potential unrealized. We built this program to give African creatives what the industry actually demands: practical skills, real experience, and a direct path to opportunity.

Our instructors are working professionals from Effex Studios. Our curriculum is built around live projects. And our graduates don't just leave with certificates — they leave with portfolios, connections, and in many cases, job offers.

This is not a traditional classroom. This is where you become a professional.

"We don't teach theory. We teach the work."
The Foundation

Three pillars. One mission.

01

Real Skills

Industry-standard tools and techniques. Camera operation, lighting, editing, color grading, sound design — taught by professionals who use them daily. No outdated curriculum. No busywork.

02

Real Projects

From your first week, you work on actual briefs. Short films, brand content, documentary segments — real deliverables for real audiences. Your portfolio starts building immediately.

03

Real Opportunities

Top-performing graduates enter the Effex Studios talent pipeline. Others leave with industry connections, references, and the skills to freelance or launch their own ventures.

What you'll learn.

Videography & Cinematography

Camera systems, shot composition, movement, lighting for video. From run-and-gun to cinematic setups.

Photography

Intensive · 8 weeks
