Frequently Asked Questions

There are no formal prerequisites. We accept beginners with genuine passion for creative media, as well as those with some experience looking to level up. What matters most is your commitment to learning and putting in the work. You should have access to a computer for editing courses, but camera equipment is provided during training.
Tuition varies by program. We offer flexible payment plans and, in some cases, partial scholarships for exceptional candidates with financial need. Contact us directly for current pricing and payment options. We believe financial constraints shouldn't prevent talented individuals from accessing quality training.
All programs are held in-person at our facilities in Douala, Cameroon. We believe hands-on training requires physical presence, access to professional equipment, and direct mentorship. Some supplementary materials may be provided online, but core instruction happens in our studio.
Graduates leave with a professional portfolio of real work completed during the program. Top performers are considered for the Effex Studios talent pipeline, with opportunities to work on actual client projects. All graduates gain access to our alumni network and ongoing mentorship opportunities.
Yes. Many students complete one program and return for another to expand their skill set. We offer discounts for returning students. Some students combine programs — for example, Videography followed by Editing — to become more versatile professionals.
