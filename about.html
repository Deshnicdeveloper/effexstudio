Our Story

About Effex Studios

We started with a camera and a conviction: African stories deserve world-class production.

Effex Studios was born in Douala, Cameroon, a city of hustle, creativity, and untold stories. We saw a gap: incredible stories all around us, but not enough people telling them with the craft and care they deserved.

So we built a studio to change that. Not a studio that imports foreign aesthetics, but one that understands the rhythm, the light, the soul of this place. One that makes Africa look the way it feels.

Today, we work with brands, organizations, and visionaries across the continent and beyond. From brand films to documentaries, commercial photography to social content — everything we create has one thing in common: it tells the truth, beautifully.

"Every untold story deserves a camera. Every raw talent deserves a chance."

Where We Are

Based in Douala, Cameroon

Our home is Douala — Cameroon's economic capital, a city of 3 million people, endless creative energy, and stories waiting to be told. From here, we serve clients across Africa and the world.

We believe location shapes perspective. Being rooted in Central Africa gives us access to stories, talent, and a visual language that you won't find anywhere else.
